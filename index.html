Frequently Asked Questions

What format does discal.xyz provide?

A standard iCalendar (.ics) feed compatible with major calendar applications.

Do I need to recreate events elsewhere?

No. Continue using Discord Events as normal.

Do calendars update automatically?

Yes. Subscribers automatically receive event updates through their calendar application.

Can non-Discord users subscribe?

Yes. Anyone with the calendar feed URL can subscribe from a compatible calendar app.
