Terms of Service

discal.xyz is a Discord bot that generates calendar (iCalendar .ics) feeds from Discord Events.

You agree to use the service as-is. Features may change or stop at any time without notice.

No data is stored. We do not use a database, logs, or persistent storage of any kind.

Calendar feed URLs can be accessed by anyone who has them. Users are responsible for how they share them.

We are not responsible for missed events, sync issues, or third-party calendar applications.

Privacy Policy

We do not collect or store personal data.

This includes Discord IDs, server IDs, messages, events, IP addresses, or any identifying information.

All processing happens in real time only to generate calendar feeds, then is immediately discarded.

No logging system or analytics system exists.

Calendar feed URLs may be accessed by anyone who has them and should be treated as private links.

Third-party calendar apps (Google, Apple, Outlook, etc.) may apply their own privacy policies.
